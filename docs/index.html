Why PORTLUV?

PORTLUV is a website dedicated for professional designers, people who are putting all of their effort into their designs. We keep their portfolio's safe whilst giving these professional designers a profile worth showing.

Mission

Our mission is to support professional designers by providing an online portfolio service and helping them find their jobs.

Goals

Our goal is to make professional designers have the coolest portfolio collection and to find these professionals equally cool jobs on our platform.

Connections

Every single thing in our lives is connected in some form or another. Your toaster is connected to electricity, your sink is connected to the plumbing system, your phone is connected to the internet, and so on.

Our idea is inspired by these connections. For something to be useful, it has to be connected to the right entity. Connecting someone to the right people is essential for their future. All it takes is the right medium of communication between two sides, and that’s what we strive to be.

FAQ

Frequently Ask Questions

What is PORTLUV?

PORTLUV is an app that supports designers to express their creativity by sharing their design portfolio online on our platform. PORTLUV works as an online portfolio sharing platform and as a connector between professional designers and their future jobs.

How do I use this app?

Simply access the website, then you can start uploading your designs on the dashboard. Feel free to explore the website, as it is designed to provide comfort for users. If you have any difficulties, scroll down to the bottom, and find the help page. We would be more than happy to assist you.

Is this available for everyone?

Yes. PORTLUV is available for everyone from any country to access.

How much would PORTLUV cost?

More or less Free! PORTLUV would not charge you for registering or uploading your designs. However, you have a storage capacity limit on your account. Thus if you want to increase the storage limit, you have to pay some amount of price.

I’m having difficulties, what do I do?

Feel free to contact us at our website. Scroll down to the bottom of any page for access to the terms and conditions, contact support, or anything that would be useful for you. Alternatively, you can contact our admin at support@infund.com.
